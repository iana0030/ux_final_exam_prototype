Shrimp ramen

(217)

8

Ingredients

20

Minutes

375

Calories

Servings

Weight Conversion

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • Shrimps
  • Peas
  • Black sesame seeds
  • Cilantro
  • Oil

AD

Directions

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Peel and fry the shrimps
  3. Boil and fry the peas
  4. Boil water over medium heat on stove
  5. Cook egg
  6. Cook noodles
  7. Add egg, peas and shrimps to noodles
  8. Garnish with sesame seeds and cilantro
