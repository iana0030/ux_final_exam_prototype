Veg Burger

(20)

10

Ingredients

35

Minutes

400

Calories

Servings

Weight Conversion

Ingredients

  • 5 burger buns
  • 5 potatoes
  • 5 cheese slices
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 carrot
  • 3 Tbsp. sweet corn kernels (optional)
  • 100 grams cabbage
  • 1 Tbsp. butter
  • 2 tsp. oil
  • Salt (as per taste)
  • Black pepper (Powder)
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cucumber

AD

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
  2. Add the onions, carrot, capsicum and saute for a minute or two.
  3. Remove and add this to the mashed potatoes.
  4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper powder.
  5. Mix well and shape the mixture into 4 to 5 round patties.
  6. Heat little butter in a nonstick pan.
  7. Add the prepared patties and fry for 30 seconds on both sides or until lightly browned
  8. Add a cheese slice on top, cover with a lid and cook for another 30 seconds or until itstarts to melt.
  9. Lightly warm the burger buns and spread butter on one half of the burger buns.
  10. Add some cabbage, 1 tomato, cucumber and onion slice on top.
  11. Place the cooked burger patty with the cheese on top.
  12. Sprinkle a bit of pepper powder and top up with some sauce if desired.
  13. Cover with the other half of the bun.
  14. Serve with french fries.
